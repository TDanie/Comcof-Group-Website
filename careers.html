Who We Are

A Company Worth
Building

Comcof Group is a coffee company focused on building long-term capabilities across sourcing, trade, market access, finance coordination, quality, processing, and value addition.

We believe respected coffee companies are built through disciplined execution, strong relationships, reliable systems, consistent quality, and a clear understanding of global markets.

We are building the team and infrastructure required to participate competitively in the future of coffee. We are still early. That is precisely the opportunity.

"Comcof is building its first generation of leaders. Joining at this stage means helping establish the systems, standards, culture, and operating discipline that will shape the company for years to come."
A Message from the Founding Team

We are looking for people who are dependable, commercially aware, detail-oriented, ethical, and prepared to take responsibility.
