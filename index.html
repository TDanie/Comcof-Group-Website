Who We Are

Building a New Standard in
Global Coffee Commerce

Comcof Group is being built as a vertically integrated coffee multinational, designed to create sustainable value across the entire chain, from producing origins to the world's most demanding markets.

The name says it all: Comcof is the commerce of coffee. We are not a café group or a single-origin boutique. We are building a serious commercial enterprise, step by deliberate step, with the systems and long-term ambition to participate across every dimension of the global coffee economy.

Rooted in East Africa's coffee landscapes, anchored by Uganda as our home origin, and built with global commercial markets in view, Comcof Group is designed for the next 50 years.

Business Architecture

The Full Value Chain

Our model is designed to capture value at every stage, from the coffee farm to the final cup. Some stages are active today; others are being built, deliberately and in order.

01
Origin Sourcing
Direct relationships with growers and cooperatives in Uganda, with the wider East African belt to follow. Building traceable supply chains from the ground up.
Active
02
Processing & Value Addition
Hulling, sorting, and grading through established partner mills today, with our own capacity planned at origin. Capturing value before export.
Via Partners
03
Green Coffee Trading
International trade in Ugandan green coffee, spot and forward. Building the market knowledge and buyer relationships this trade demands.
Active
04
Warehousing & Logistics
Freight and storage handled with established logistics partners today. Our own warehousing in key commercial hubs is planned for later phases.
Via Partners
05
Roasting & Packaging
A planned roasting and packaging arm for private-label and branded production. In development, not yet in operation.
Planned
06
Distribution
Planned B2B and B2C distribution across target markets: hospitality, retail chains, food service, and specialty channels.
Planned
07
Branded Products
A planned portfolio of branded coffee products for premium consumers, carrying African provenance as a brand asset.
Planned
08
Retail & Experiences
Premium retail environments and coffee experiences, envisioned for later phases. The future consumer face of the Comcof brand.
Planned
09
Market Intelligence
Market data and trade analysis that inform commercial decisions across the group, built in-house as we trade.
