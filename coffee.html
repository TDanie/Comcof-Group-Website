Quality Framework

Our Commitment to Quality

Traceable Origin

We record traceability data for every lot we source: farm or cooperative, region, process method, and harvest date.

Cupping Standards

Lots are cupped against SCA protocol, and we will not call a coffee specialty without independent Q-grader verification.

Export-Grade Prep

Green coffee prepared to destination market specification. Moisture, defect count, screen size, and bag presentation to international standard.

Private Label (In Development)

We are developing white-label and private-label capability for roasted and packaged coffee, from roast profile development to packaging and fulfilment.
